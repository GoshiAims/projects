> History_

jan 2021 - founded
berserk originally started as a consulting and prototyping service for expert advisors. These are bots that allow you to interact with the metatrader application programmatically to do FX trading.

june 2022 - change in direction
berserk transitioned over to a mobile tech support service, providing on-site troubleshooting, repairs and IT assistance for individuals and business'.

may 2025 - next step
berserk is planning to incorporate within the next few weeks and go to the next level, this includes a social media presence and possibly expanding into game development.
